Collaborating for Growth

We are proud to partner with these fantastic organisations to further our mission and support our community garden:

The Urban Co-op PAUL Partnership Limerick Youth Service Living Woodlands Limerick City and County Council I Love Limerick

These collaborations are vital to the success and sustainability of our community garden and its initiatives.

Our Community Garden: A Collaborative Space

Our community garden is at the heart of our efforts, a vibrant space where individuals, local groups, and our partner organisations come together to cultivate, learn, and connect with nature.

Through the support of our partners, we are able to provide resources, workshops, and opportunities for everyone to get involved in growing fresh produce, fostering biodiversity, and building a stronger community.

We believe in the power of collaboration to create a thriving and sustainable environment for all.
