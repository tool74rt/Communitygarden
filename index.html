The Secret Garden

Welcome

The mission of The Secret Garden is to cultivate a welcoming and inclusive environment where community members can come together to grow their own food, learn about sustainable gardening practices, and connect with one another. The garden aims to promote environmental stewardship, enhance biodiversity, and provide educational opportunities that empower individuals to take an active role in their local ecosystem. By fostering a sense of belonging and community spirit, The Secret Garden seeks to improve the quality of life for residents in Limerick.
