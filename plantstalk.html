Previous Gardening Questions & Answers

How often should I water my succulents?

Water succulents sparingly, typically every 2-4 weeks, allowing the soil to dry out completely between waterings.

What's the best soil for growing tomatoes in pots?

Use a high-quality potting mix specifically formulated for vegetables, ensuring good drainage.

Why are the leaves on my peace lily turning yellow?

Yellowing leaves can be due to overwatering, underwatering, or lack of nutrients. Check the soil moisture and consider fertilizing.

How do I get rid of aphids on my rose bushes?

Try spraying them with a strong jet of water, using insecticidal soap, or introducing natural predators like ladybugs.

When is the best time to prune my hydrangea?

Pruning time depends on the type of hydrangea. For most, prune after the flowers fade in late summer or early fall.

What are some low-light indoor plants?

Snake plants, ZZ plants, pothos, and peace lilies are known to tolerate low light conditions.

How can I improve the drainage in my garden soil?

Amend the soil with organic matter like compost or add perlite or sand to improve drainage.

What's the best way to propagate herbs like basil?

Basil can be easily propagated by taking stem cuttings and placing them in water until roots develop.
