Garden Coordinator

The Garden Coordinator serves as the primary leader and organizer of the community garden. This role involves overseeing daily operations, planning garden activities, and ensuring that all members are informed about upcoming events and meetings. The Coordinator also acts as a liaison between the garden and local organizations, helping to secure resources and support for the garden’s initiatives.

Magda Cyran

Educational Outreach Coordinator

The Educational Outreach Coordinator focuses on developing and implementing educational programs and workshops for garden members and the wider community. This role involves creating lesson plans, organizing events, and facilitating hands-on learning experiences related to gardening, sustainability, and healthy eating. The Coordinator also collaborates with local schools and organizations to promote gardening education and engage new members.

Sean Bresnihan

Plot Steward

Plot Stewards are responsible for maintaining their designated plots within the garden. This includes planting, watering, weeding, and harvesting crops as well as keeping the area tidy and healthy. Stewards are encouraged to share their gardening knowledge with other members and participate in group activities. They play a vital role in ensuring that the garden remains productive and visually appealing.

Helen Flanagan

Volunteer Coordinator

The Volunteer Coordinator recruits and manages volunteers who assist with various garden tasks and events. This role involves organizing volunteer days, training new volunteers, and ensuring that all tasks are completed efficiently. The Volunteer Coordinator helps foster a sense of community among volunteers and encourages participation in garden projects, such as workshops or community outreach initiatives.

About Our Organization

Welcome to Our Community Garden!

At The Secret Garden, we believe in the power of nature to bring people together. Established in 2003, our garden serves as a vibrant green space where community members can cultivate not only plants but also friendships, knowledge, and a sense of belonging. Located in the heart of Limerick, our garden is a sanctuary for those who wish to connect with the earth and each other.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote sustainable gardening practices while fostering community engagement and environmental stewardship. We aim to provide a welcoming space where individuals and families can learn about gardening, grow their own food, and participate in workshops and events. By cultivating our garden, we cultivate our community—nurturing relationships and encouraging healthy lifestyles.

Sustainable Practices

We are committed to sustainable gardening practices that respect our environment. Our garden utilizes organic methods, composting, and water conservation techniques to minimize our ecological footprint. We believe that by growing food in harmony with nature, we can create a healthier ecosystem for future generations. We also host educational programs to teach our members about permaculture, biodiversity, and the importance of local food systems.
