Champion School Community Plants 2,500 Trees on Its Limerick Campus

How did this school plant 2,504 trees in a year? Since the film was made in 2022 the school has added another 1,000 trees to its campus grounds. The Easy Treesie Project has the aim of planting 1M Trees with Ireland's 1M school children as part of the global UNEP-backed initiative to plant a trillion trees during the UN Decade on Ecosystem Restoration. The Coláiste Chiaráin community, Croom, Co. Limerick has run an extraordinary initiative, in partnership with many including the Tree Council of Ireland and with funding from Rethink Ireland, the Government of Ireland Dept. of Agriculture Food and the Marine and Coillte in collaboration with registered charity Crann, Trees for Ireland and our many supporters.

Creating an organic garden in an Irish hotel

This week on Organic Matters, we speak to Mary Fitzgerald and her head gardener Caoimhín Brosnan at the Woodlands House Hotel in Co Limerick. The pair discuss how they designed and created an organic garden and the role the garden plays in supplying food to the hotel. Mary also discusses her plans to have at least one fully organic meal on their menu by the end of this year.
